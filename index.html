Linkedin Ninja

Linkedin Ninja bookmarklet enhances the Linkedin experience by introducing filtering & sorting to Linkedin's 'People you may know' list by number of connections & keyword

Bookmarklet

The Linkedin Ninja bookmarklet is a special type of bookmark that you can put in your bookmark toolbar. It will allow you to introduce much needed features to the Linkedin experience.

Features

  • Load 100+ profiles by clicking a button
  • Filter results by keyword (name & job title)
  • Sort by number of shared connections

Install

To install, simply drag the link below into your bookmark toolbar.

Linkedin Ninja

Use

Load the features/enhancements by clicking on the bookmarklet when on the 'People you may know' page.
